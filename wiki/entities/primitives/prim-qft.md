## Summary

The Quantum Fourier Transform (QFT) is a quantum analog of the discrete Fourier transform.

## Definition

QFT acts on n qubits and performs the transform: |j⟩ → (1/√N) Σ_k e^(2πijk/N) |k⟩

## Complexity

- **Gate Count**: O(n²)
- **Depth**: O(n)
- **Qubits**: n
